Thomasina Miers' potato and chickpea curry recipe

A hearty, comforting curry that's perfect for a summer's evening
Serves 4-6
PT30M
PT40M
PT1H10M
  • 2 large onions, peeled and chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, peeled and minced
  • 2 medium-hot red chillies, seeded and chopped
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 2 large potatoes, peeled and cut into 2.5cm chunks
  • 2 x 400g cans chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 tbsp tomato puree
  • 400ml coconut milk
  • 200ml water
  • Fresh coriander leaves, chopped (optional)
  1. Heat the oil in a large saucepan over a medium heat. Add the onions and cook, stirring occasionally, until they're softened and lightly browned (about 10-12 minutes).
  2. Add the garlic, chillies, cumin, coriander, turmeric, garam masala, salt and pepper. Cook, stirring constantly, for 1-2 minutes.
  3. Add the potatoes and cook, stirring occasionally, for 5 minutes.
  4. Stir in the chickpeas, tomato puree, coconut milk and water. Bring to a simmer, then reduce the heat to low and cook, covered, for 20-25 minutes, or until the potatoes are tender.
  5. Taste and adjust the seasoning as needed. Serve hot, garnished with chopped coriander leaves if desired.
